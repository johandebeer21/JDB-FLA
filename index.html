Disclaimer & Legal Information

Firearm license applications are subject to approval by the South African Police Service (SAPS). Submission of documents and assistance does not guarantee license issuance. Applicants must meet all legal requirements and comply with the Firearms Control Act, 2000.

We ensure all documentation complies with current legislation and SAPS standards. However, applicants remain responsible for truthful and complete information submitted to SAPS.
